What is Sahaayukt ?

Sahaayukt is India's one of a kind crowdfunding platform that allows donors to directly donate towards products that are required by the NGO. In the last 4 years, we have partnered with 1000+ NGOs all over India to raise 100+ crores worth of products. More than 5 lakh unique donors have used our platform to donate to charities of their choice. As a donor, you are guaranteed 100% transparency - after you donate towards the products, you will receive an update from the campaigner.

Communication Forum

Diffrent social welfare clubs from different colleges can communicate with each other via our chat forum to organise events together at various Non profits.Our objective is to help the process of inculcation of philanthropic values in teenagers.

Volunteering

Sahaayukt powers volunteering in India. And of course, we depend on several volunteers who power Sahaayukt itself. Over the years, many have contributed to Sahaayukt's services, some even joined the Sahaayukt team and moved on to become Sahaayukt ambassadors for life.
